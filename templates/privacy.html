Your Privacy Matters: CalcMaster Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our feedback form or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Feedback or message content

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, calculators used, time spent on site
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring URLs

1.3 Calculation Data

Important: We do NOT store, save, or transmit any data you enter into our calculators. All calculations are performed locally in your browser, and your input data is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our calculator services
  • Responding to your inquiries and feedback
  • Analyzing website usage to improve user experience
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

3.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your experience on our website.

3.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to display relevant ads (e.g., Google AdSense)

3.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: Google Ads Settings

4.3 Google Forms

Our feedback form is powered by Google Forms. Information submitted through the form is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connection
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

  • No storage of calculation data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics retention policies
  • Calculation Data: Never stored or retained
